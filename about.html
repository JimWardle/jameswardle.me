About Me

I'm James, a DevOps Engineer based in the West Midlands with an unusual background that makes me good at what I do.

My Story

I didn't start in tech. I studied Communication and Media at Coventry University, then spent 5 years teaching ICT and Computing to teenagers. Teaching 15-year-olds about databases and programming taught me:

  • How to explain complex things simply – If you can make a reluctant Year 10 understand SQL joins, you can help any developer understand your deployment process
  • Patience and persistence – Some problems need multiple approaches before they click
  • How to document things properly – Because if you don't, you'll be answering the same questions forever

What Drives Me

I genuinely enjoy solving problems that make other people's work easier. Whether that's:

  • Automating manual deployments so developers can focus on building features
  • Setting up monitoring that actually helps rather than just creating noise
  • Writing runbooks that new team members can actually follow
  • Building tools that save hours of tedious work

How I Work

My teaching background shows up in how I approach DevOps:

  • I explain things clearly – No jargon, no assumptions about what people already know
  • I document as I go – Future me (and future colleagues) will thank present me
  • I break complex problems down – Big scary infrastructure changes become manageable steps
  • I'm not precious about my solutions – If there's a better way, let's do that instead

Beyond Work

I live in the West Midlands with my wife (who keeps me grounded and gives excellent website feedback). When I'm not automating deployments, I'm usually:

  • Building side projects that solve real problems I actually have
  • Learning new tech by building something useful with it
  • Playing board games that require more strategy than luck
  • Trying to convince people that pineapple absolutely belongs on pizza

The bottom line: I'm someone who makes technical teams more effective by combining solid engineering skills with the ability to communicate clearly and help others succeed.
