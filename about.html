About Me

I'm a DevOps Engineer with more than 15 years of experience in IT, now focused on automation, Azure, and platform engineering. My day-to-day work is about making deployments faster, infrastructure more reliable, and teams more productive.

I’ve led the move to scalable infrastructure using Azure VM Scale Sets, built automated pipelines that eliminate repetitive tasks, and mentored engineers while documenting and improving internal processes. I work best when I’m close to the code, helping developers ship better software, and simplifying complexity without cutting corners.

My background includes time as a software engineer, educator, and systems technician — and that blend shows in how I work: pragmatic, structured, and good at explaining tricky things clearly. I’ve worked across public and private sectors, from universities to SaaS platforms.

If your team values clean automation, solid engineering, and a bias toward action, we’ll probably get on well.
